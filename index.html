What this means for your operation

Owner churn is usually invisible until it has already happened, an owner does not announce they are shopping around, they just quietly leave when the contract allows. The operators who track revenue delivered against revenue promised, and who ask owners directly rather than assuming silence means satisfaction, are the ones who catch the pattern before it costs them the property.
